About Gurii

Premium products, honest prices, delivered across the UK.

Gurii was born from a simple idea: modern living deserves thoughtfully curated gear that doesn't break the bank. We believe that innovative health monitors, dependable automotive tech, immersive audio, and delightful collectibles should be accessible to everyone in the United Kingdom.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Gurii started as a small online passion project. Today we serve thousands of happy customers from London to Glasgow, offering a carefully selected range of products that blend function, style, and value. Every item in our catalogue goes through a rigorous quality check – because we only sell what we’d happily use ourselves.

Our Values

  • Quality first: We partner with trusted suppliers and test products before they reach you.
  • Fair pricing: No hidden costs, no inflated RRPs. You see a fair price, not a gimmick.
  • UK-based support: Real people, real help. Call or email us – we reply fast.
  • Fast delivery: Free standard UK delivery on orders over £40, with most items dispatched within 24 hours.
